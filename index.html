Dot features  

Dot is the first app of its kind that allows college students to safely buy and sell with each other on campus. All users are veified by their .edu email address which allows us to know the identity of each user making your online shopping experience safer.

Dot is starting out at the University Of Pittsburgh and branching out to surrounding city schools. If you want to use dot on your campus please contact us and let us know!

How it Works

Join A Campus

Sign up with your .edu email address to connect with students near you.

Take A & Picture

Snap a photo of your item and enter some quick info to post.

Find A Buyer

Get notified of buyers with similar needs by DotList and message them to confirm any extra details

Meetup Safely

Meet the buyer in a safe and secure campus location and collect money for your item
